# Customer Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end Customer Behavior Analysis project using Python, SQL Server, and Power BI to uncover customer purchasing patterns, spending behavior, subscription trends, and product preferences. The project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, SQL-based business reporting, and interactive dashboards.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A leading retail company wants to better understand its customers shopping behavior in order to improve sales, customer satisfaction and long term loyalty. The management team has noticed changes in purchasing across demographics, product categories and sales channels (online vs offline). They are particularly interested in uncovering which factors, such as discounts, reviews, seasons or payment preferences, drive consumer decisions and repeat purchases. The task is to analyze the company's consumer behavior dataset to answer the following overarching business question:-
"How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement and optimize marketing and product strategies?"

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* SQL Server
* Power BI
* Jupyter Notebook

## 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains:

* 3,900 customer purchase records
* 18 features
* Customer demographics
* Purchase details
* Subscription information
* Discount and promotion usage
* Product ratings and shopping behavior

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)

* Handled missing values
* Standardized column names
* Created age groups and derived features
* Performed data consistency checks
* Loaded cleaned data into SQL Server

### 2. Business Analysis (SQL Server)

Key business questions explored:

* Revenue by gender
* Subscriber vs non-subscriber spending
* High-spending discount users
* Top-rated products
* Shipping preference analysis
* Customer segmentation
* Repeat buyer behavior
* Revenue contribution by age group

### 3. Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Built an interactive dashboard to visualize:

* Customer spending patterns
* Revenue distribution
* Subscription performance
* Product insights
* Customer segments
* Shopping behavior trends

## 📊 Key Insights

* Subscribers generated higher overall business value compared to non-subscribers.
* Loyal customers contributed significantly to revenue growth.
* Certain products consistently received higher ratings and purchase volumes.
* Discount usage influenced purchasing behavior across customer segments.
* Age groups and shipping preferences showed distinct spending patterns.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Strengthen subscription programs through exclusive benefits.
* Implement loyalty programs to increase customer retention.
* Optimize discount strategies to balance sales and profitability.
* Promote top-performing products through targeted campaigns.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
